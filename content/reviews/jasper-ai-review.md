## What Is Jasper AI?

Jasper AI is an AI writing assistant designed for marketers, bloggers, and agencies. It uses GPT-4 under the hood with custom templates for marketing copy.

- Brand Voice training
- SEO mode with Surfer integration

## Pros & Cons

- Great for teams with Brand Voice

**Cons:**
- Expensive compared to ChatGPT Plus
