## What Is Otter.ai?
Otter.ai is an artificial intelligence-powered transcription tool designed to help users convert audio and video recordings into text. It utilizes automatic speech recognition technology to provide fast and accurate transcriptions, making it a valuable resource for freelancers, agencies, and developers. With its user-friendly interface and robust features, Otter.ai aims to streamline the transcription process and increase productivity.
