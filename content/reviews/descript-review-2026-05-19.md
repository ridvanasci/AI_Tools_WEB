## What Is Descript?
Descript is an innovative AI-powered audio and video editing tool designed to simplify the post-production process for creators. It offers a range of features, including automatic transcription, multi-track editing, and screen recording, making it an attractive option for freelancers, agencies, and developers. By leveraging AI technology, Descript aims to reduce editing time and improve overall productivity.

## Key Features
* Automatic transcription: Descript uses AI to transcribe audio and video files, allowing for easy editing and subtitling.
* Multi-track editing: The tool enables users to edit multiple audio and video tracks simultaneously, streamlining the post-production process.
* Screen recording: Descript offers a built-in screen recording feature, making it easy to capture video and audio content.
* Overdub: The tool's Overdub feature allows users to re-record sections of audio with ease, ensuring high-quality sound.
* Collaboration: Descript enables real-time collaboration, allowing multiple users to work on a project simultaneously.
