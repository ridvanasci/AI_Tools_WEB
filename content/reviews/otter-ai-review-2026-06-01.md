## What Is Otter.ai?
Otter.ai is a cutting-edge AI-powered transcription tool designed to automatically generate transcripts from audio and video recordings. This innovative platform utilizes artificial intelligence to recognize spoken words, identify speakers, and provide highly accurate transcripts. Otter.ai is particularly useful for freelancers, agencies, and developers who need to transcribe meetings, interviews, lectures, or podcasts.

## Key Features
* Automatic transcription: Otter.ai can automatically transcribe audio and video recordings with high accuracy.
* Speaker identification: The platform can identify different speakers in a recording and label them accordingly.
* Real-time transcription: Otter.ai can transcribe recordings in real-time, making it ideal for live events or meetings.
* Customizable vocabulary: Users can add custom words and phrases to improve transcription accuracy.
* Collaboration tools: Otter.ai allows multiple users to collaborate on transcripts, making it easy to edit and share transcripts.
