## What Is Otter.ai?
Otter.ai is an AI-powered transcription tool designed to help users convert audio and video files into text quickly and accurately. It supports various file formats, including MP3, MP4, and WAV, and can also record and transcribe conversations in real-time. This makes it a valuable tool for freelancers, agencies, and developers who need to transcribe interviews, meetings, or other audio content.

## Key Features
* Accurate transcription: Otter.ai uses AI to transcribe audio and video files with high accuracy, even in noisy environments.
* Real-time transcription: Otter.ai can record and transcribe conversations in real-time, making it ideal for meetings, interviews, and lectures.
* Multi-language support: Otter.ai supports transcription in multiple languages, including Spanish, French, German, and many more.
