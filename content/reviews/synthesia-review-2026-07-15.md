## What Is Synthesia?
Synthesia is an AI-powered video creation tool that allows users to create customized video avatars for various applications, including explainer videos, social media content, and e-learning materials. It utilizes artificial intelligence to generate realistic avatar videos from text-based inputs, enabling users to produce high-quality video content quickly and efficiently. With its user-friendly interface and extensive range of features, Synthesia has become a popular choice among freelancers, agencies, and developers.
