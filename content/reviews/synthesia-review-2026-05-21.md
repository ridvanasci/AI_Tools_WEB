## What Is Synthesia?
Synthesia is an AI-powered video creation platform that allows users to create customized video avatars, also known as digital humans, for various applications such as explainer videos, training content, and social media clips. This innovative tool utilizes artificial intelligence to generate realistic and engaging videos with minimal input required from the user. By leveraging Synthesia, users can save time and resources while producing high-quality video content.
