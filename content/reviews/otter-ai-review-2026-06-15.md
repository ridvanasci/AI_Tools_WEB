## What Is Otter.ai?
Otter.ai is an AI-powered transcription tool that uses automatic speech recognition to generate transcripts of audio and video recordings. It is designed to help individuals, businesses, and organizations save time and increase productivity by providing accurate and readable transcripts. Otter.ai can be used for a variety of purposes, including interviews, meetings, lectures, and podcasts.

## Key Features
* Automatic speech recognition with high accuracy rates
* Support for multiple audio and video file formats
* Real-time transcription and live captioning
* Ability to edit and annotate transcripts

## Who Is It Best For?
