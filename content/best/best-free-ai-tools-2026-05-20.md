## Top 5 Free AI Tools

### 1. ChatGPT (Free Tier)
The most versatile AI assistant available. The free tier gives access to GPT-4o with daily limits — enough for most casual users.
- **Key features:** Writing, coding, analysis, image generation
- **Pricing:** Free (limited) / $20/mo for Plus
- **Best for:** General purpose AI tasks

### 2. Claude (Free Tier)
Anthropic's Claude is the best free AI for long-form writing and document analysis. The 200K context window is available even on free tier.
- **Key features:** Long documents, nuanced writing, coding
- **Pricing:** Free (limited) / $20/mo for Pro
- **Best for:** Writers, researchers, analysts

### 3. Perplexity AI
The best free AI search engine. Unlike Google, it gives direct answers with cited sources — no ads, no SEO spam.
- **Key features:** Real-time web search, cited answers, follow-up questions
- **Pricing:** Free (unlimited basic) / $20/mo for Pro
- **Best for:** Research, fact-checking, quick answers

### 4. Canva AI (Free Tier)
Canva's AI tools — Magic Write, text-to-image, background remover — are available on the free plan.
- **Key features:** AI image generation, design templates, Magic Write
- **Pricing:** Free (limited AI credits) / $15/mo for Pro
- **Best for:** Social media graphics, presentations

### 5. GitHub Copilot (Free Tier)
GitHub Copilot now has a free tier with 2,000 code completions per month — enough for hobby developers.
- **Key features:** Code completion, chat, multi-language support
- **Pricing:** Free (2K completions/mo) / $10/mo for Pro
- **Best for:** Developers, students

## How to Choose

Pick based on your primary use case: **ChatGPT** for general tasks, **Claude** for writing, **Perplexity** for research, **Canva AI** for design, **Copilot** for coding. All are free to start — upgrade only when you hit limits.
